Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the registration fees? Registration fees vary by attendee type, with early bird discounts available. Here are the current rates:
    • Standard Pass: $300 (Early Bird: $250 until June 30)
    • Student Pass: $150 (Valid student ID required)
    • Group Discounts: 10% off for groups of 5 or more
  • Can I attend virtually? Yes, TechFusion 2024 offers comprehensive virtual attendance options. Virtual attendees will have access to live streams of all sessions, interactive Q&A sessions, virtual networking rooms, and downloadable resources.
  • What COVID-19 precautions are being taken? The health and safety of our attendees, speakers, and staff are our top priorities. We have implemented the following measures:
    • Mandatory Mask Policy: Masks must be worn at all times indoors.
    • Social Distancing: Seating arrangements and event spaces have been adjusted to ensure social distancing.
    • Sanitization Stations: Hand sanitizing stations are available throughout the venue.
    • Temperature Checks: Temperature checks will be conducted at entry points.
    • Hybrid Options: Attendees have the option to switch to virtual attendance if they feel unwell or prefer remote participation.
  • Is there a code of conduct for attendees? Yes, all attendees are expected to adhere to our code of conduct, which promotes respect, inclusivity, and professional behavior. Any violations may result in removal from the event without a refund.
  • Are meals provided during the conference? Yes, full-day attendees will have access to complimentary breakfast, lunch, and refreshments. Special dietary requests can be accommodated upon prior notification.
